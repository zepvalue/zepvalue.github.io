# Privacy Policy

<p class="updated">media_sync · Last updated: 16 June 2026</p>

media_sync ("the app", "we", "us") is a personal, local tool that lets a creator
republish their own short-form videos to TikTok. This Privacy Policy explains what
information the app accesses, how it is used, and your choices. The app is operated
by Giuseppe V. You can reach us at [zepvalue@gmail.com](mailto:zepvalue@gmail.com).

By connecting your TikTok account to media_sync, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

## What the app does

media_sync runs locally on your own computer. After you authenticate with your own
TikTok account, you select one of your own video files and the app delivers it to
your TikTok inbox as a draft. You then review and finalize the post inside the
TikTok app. The app never publishes content on your behalf without you completing
that step in TikTok.

## Information we access

We access the minimum information needed to perform the action you request:

- **TikTok account confirmation (`user.info.basic`).** When you sign in through
  TikTok's Login Kit (OAuth), we read basic profile information — your display name
  and account identifier — solely to confirm which account is authenticated and to
  display the connected username so you can confirm before posting. No other profile
  data is requested, used, or stored.
- **OAuth access token.** TikTok returns a token that authorizes the app to act on
  your behalf for the scopes you approve (`user.info.basic`, `video.upload`). The
  token is held only on your device for the duration of your session and is used
  exclusively to call TikTok's API.
- **The video you select.** The MP4 file you choose is uploaded directly from your
  device to your own TikTok inbox using TikTok's Content Posting API
  (`/v2/post/publish/inbox/video/init/`). We do not keep a copy of your videos.

## Information we do NOT collect

- We do **not** collect analytics, usage tracking, advertising identifiers, or
  location data.
- We do **not** sell, rent, or share your information with any third party for
  marketing.
- We do **not** access, post, or process any content that is not your own. No
  third-party or scraped content is ever uploaded.

## How information is used

The information above is used only to:

1. Confirm the authenticated TikTok account and show you the connected username.
2. Upload the video you select to your own TikTok inbox at your request.

Every action is initiated by you, the authenticated user, on your own content.

## Sharing and disclosure

media_sync communicates only with **TikTok's official API** in order to perform the
actions you initiate. Your interaction with TikTok is also governed by
[TikTok's Privacy Policy](https://www.tiktok.com/legal/page/row/privacy-policy/en).
We do not transmit your data to any other service.

## Data retention

Because media_sync is a local tool, any access token is stored only on your device
and only for as long as your session lasts. We operate no server that stores your
personal data. You can revoke the app's access at any time from your
[TikTok account settings](https://www.tiktok.com/setting) → *Security & permissions
→ Manage app permissions*. Revoking access immediately prevents further use of the
token.

## Your rights

You may disconnect media_sync from your TikTok account at any time, which ends all
access. If you have questions about your data, contact us at
[zepvalue@gmail.com](mailto:zepvalue@gmail.com).

## Children's privacy

media_sync is not directed to anyone under the age required to hold a TikTok account
in their jurisdiction, and we do not knowingly process data from such individuals.

## Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be
reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.
