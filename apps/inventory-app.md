# inventory-app

<p class="updated">An offline-first inventory management tool</p>

inventory-app is a personal, offline-first inventory management app built with
SvelteKit. It lets you track items, manage stock levels, and organize your
inventory — all while working seamlessly offline.

## Features

- **Offline-first.** Works without an internet connection using service workers
  and local storage.
- **Fast & responsive.** Built with SvelteKit for a snappy, modern UI.
- **Simple management.** Add, edit, and categorize items with ease.
